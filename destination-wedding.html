The Upside of : Destination Weddings

For a multitude of reasons, destination weddings are an excellent choice for many couples. To some, the financial savings over a traditional wedding are appealing; to others, it appears natural because many of their relatives will be travelling to attend the wedding anyway; to others, it can be a fantastic option if they do not have a large number of guests who will attend; and to still others, it may simply be a desire to break with tradition. Whatever the case may be, a destination wedding may be the ideal way to say "I do!”.

Destination weddings were traditionally assumed to be island or tropical gatherings. A destination wedding, on the other hand, simply indicates that you are getting married away from home. The location you select might be a town or city where your family or ancestors formerly lived, a location you have always longed to visit, or a location that appears to be perfectly suited to your big day. The options are limitless.
