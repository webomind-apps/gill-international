Cruise Vacations

Cruise Vacations

Cruise holidays provide a plethora of options for experiencing many places, while also providing a seemingly endless assortment of onboard facilities, entertainment, world-class cuisine, and more.

If you're looking for the perfect holiday that includes many places, wonderful cuisine and entertainment, and unique amenities such as pools, spas, fitness centers, and even shopping, a cruise may be the best option. Cruise ships aren't just floating hotels; they're more like floating cities, with practically everything you could want for an unforgettable holiday

As with any trip, it's important to understand what's included in your cruise cost. While features and facilities differ from one cruise company to the next, the following are often included in your fare:

  • Excellent service

  • Ship transportation to all ports

  • Dining - three meals per day

  • Bingo, gaming, nightclubs, and karaoke are examples of extracurricular activities and locations.

  • Some cruise companies, such as river and luxury cruise lines, have all-inclusive packages that include spirits, wines, specialty coffees, and a choice of shore excursion alternatives.

  • Recreational amenities include pools, whirlpools, and a fitness center.

  • Coffee and drinks are available (lemonade, apple, orange, ice tea) water and hot tea

  • Onboard entertainment

Again, cruise pricing and packages differ, but the following are often excluded from your cruise fare:

  • Boutiques or gift shops

  • Specialty dining establishments

  • Access to the internet

  • Round-trip airfare from the airport to the port

  • Specialty coffee, sodas, and alcoholic beverages are available.

  • Spa services and salon services

  • Casinos
